Phases 1 through 5 cleared the five gate conditions for the paper. The Phase 5 power-law fit over $d_B \leq 16$ returned $\alpha = -1.330$ with 95% CI $[-1.446, -1.214]$; the naive HUZ-inherited $\alpha = -1$ was rejected at $7.3\sigma$. Phase 6 extended the scan to $d_B \in \{18, 20, 24\}$, exposing visible $1/d_B$ curvature in the residuals and shifting the uncorrected estimate to $-1.38$. Phase 7 provided the analytic resolution: the exact exponent is $\alpha_H = -3/2$, with a subleading correction $1 - 1.13/d_B + 4.65/d_B^2$ that explains the apparent $-4/3$ observed over the limited Phase 5 range (§7.3 of the paper).
